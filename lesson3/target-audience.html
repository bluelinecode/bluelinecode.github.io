Target Audience

Who: Hikers, Explorers, Ultra-Runners, Outdoorsmen, First Responders, Military and any who may find themselves in the rugged outdoors.

Age: 16-65 Technology: Users are aware of modern and more advanced gadgets as well as high dollar outdoor equipment and mapping (GPS) technology. Income: Income levels vary between $40,000 to $100,000 or more annually. Motivation: Safety will be a top concern for many clients. Additional factors may include planning of clothing, gear, trip dates, and other activities available to the person.

Scenarios:

  1. What will the weather be this weekend? The site will provide basic weather data.
  2. Are there any storms that would interfere with our plans? The site provides storm warnings and notifications through the app.
  3. What gear do I need for my multi-day hike? Gear recommendations can be found through the site.
  4. Is the weather going to be good for camping and fishing? Weather including moon phases can be found on the site.
  5. We have an extraction up on the mountain, how are we going to stay safe and make sure we are able-bodied? Emergency weather reports ensure first-responder’s have reliable information to keep the crew safe.
