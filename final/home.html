Featured Local Companies

Choice Health Clinic

Choice Health is ran by Brent Taylor who has found a passion for health. Through Electrodermal Screening, he is able to find any and all of the underlying causes to your symptoms. Armed with herbal and homeopathic remedies along with diet tips, this is one local business you won't want to miss.

Basin Sports

Basin Sports has long been a supplier of mountain gear for all of the nature lovers of the basin! If it's an outdoor sport, you can bet you'll find what you need at this local shop!

Trout Creek Flies

Trout Creek Flies is servicing much of the Vernal area's recreation on the Green River. Meet with one of their knowledgable guides and hook into a trophy trout on our world renowned water! Be sure to stop by the fly shop to gear up before you head out.

Our Purpose

We exist to unite businesses in the Vernal area and strive to make our community conducive to growth. We maintain high standards to ensure that the businesses we interact with are an asset to the community we service.
