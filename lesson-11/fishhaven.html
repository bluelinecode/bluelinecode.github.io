Bear Lake Activities

With summer very well upon us, why not enjoy a dip in Bear Lake's turqouis water? Known as the 'Carribean of the Rockies' Bear Lake's subtle slope to its deepest valley provides many opportunities for swimmers to enjoy the refreshing waters split by the Idaho / Utah state line.

If swimming isn't to your liking, there are many activities to enjoy. These include: Biking, Bird Watching, Boating, Camping, Fishing, History, Learning, Paddlesports, RVing, Sailing, Snowmobiling, Volleyball, and so much more! Campgrounds are available for a fee and if you are lucky enough, you can make it up in early August in time for Raspberry days for all of your raspberry treats!

Don't let this opportunity pass you by!
