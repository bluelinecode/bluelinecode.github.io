Summer Attractions

Looking for something fun to do this summer in Soda Springs? Well then of course you'll need to stop by the Soda Springs Geyser! Spraying every hour on the hour, this magnificent geyser has captured the minds of man for centuries and holds a lot of history to be found! Sitting in the middle of town, this is one that there is not excuse to miss!

Another option close to town includes Hooper Park. This site has drawn people for over 160 years to view the crisp, clear Soda Water. Take a step back in time and imagine a time when W.H. Hooper made his summer home here. This is a trip that the whole family will surely enjoy!

This one might be a bit suprising, but a trip to Fairview Cemetary will gain you the privilege of seeing the Wagon Box Grave. This marker serves as a monument for a family of seven that was massacred nearby and burried in their own wagon box. Just a short jaunt from the geyser, this site is worth a visit!

Anyone can see that Soda Springs is rich with history and a little bit of that small town charm. Leave the stress in the big city and make Soda Springs a stop on your summer bucket list!
